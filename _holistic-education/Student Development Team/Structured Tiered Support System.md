## Structured Tiered Support System - Supporting Students with Additional Needs (SWAN)

<img src="/images/Curriculum/SDT/Structured_Tiered.jpg" style="width:100%">

  Anchor Green Primary School adopts the Tiered System of Support (TSS), a whole-school systemic framework by MOE, in providing support for students and managing schoolâ€™s resources effectively and efficiently. Special Educational Needs (SEN) Officers and School Counsellor work hand in hand with teachers to provide timely support for SWAN in their learning, behavioural and social-emotional needs.&nbsp;

*   Tier 1 is the core curriculum/ instruction provided to **ALL students** for both academic and non-academic areas. Quality Tier 1 support plays a preventative function by equipping all students with a good foundation in academic and non-academic skills.&nbsp;

*   Tier 2 is early targeted support provided to&nbsp;**SOME**&nbsp;students. SWAN are provided support in their specific areas of need (e.g., academic, behavioural, social-emotional).&nbsp;

*   Tier 3 is intensive targeted support provided to a&nbsp;**FEW**&nbsp;students. who require MORE intensive and individualised intervention.
