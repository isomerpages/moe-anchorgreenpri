### Anchor Green Primary School Open House 2026
We warmly invite parents of K2 children to visit Anchor Green Primary School and experience our vibrant learning environment firsthand. Join us to learn more about our programmes, school culture, and how we nurture confident and future-ready learners.<br><br>
Our Open House will provide opportunities to:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;•	Discover our key programmes and learning experiences <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;•	Interact with school leaders and teachers <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;•	Explore our school environment 

Open House Details<br>
Date: 2 July 2026<br>
Time: 2.30pm – 4.30pm <i>(Registration starts at 2.00pm)</i>

Due to limited capacity, each family may register up to 2 adults (excluding the child).

Please register via the QR code on the poster. Registration will close on 29 May 2026 at 5.00pm.<br>
We look forward to welcoming you and your child to Anchor Green Primary School!
