KCare Information
======================

  

### MK @ Anchor Green KCare Centre

  
Little Professors Learning Centre Pte Ltd is the appointed KCare operator at the MOE Kindergarten in Anchor Green. Our KCare team is dedicated to create a warm and nurturing environment, where children enjoy wholesome meals and participate in thoughtfully designed activities that help them learn, play, and discover.
  
**Centre Opening Hours**

*   School Term: 12.30 p.m. to 7.00 p.m. (Monday to Friday)
*   School Holiday: 7.30 a.m. to 7 p.m. (Monday to Friday)
  
**KCare Fees**

*   SC: $210 (Fee shown is after $150 basic subsidy)
*   PR/Foreigner: $360

KCare Additional Subsidy is provided in addition to the basic subsidy. Please follow this link for more information: https://moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten/kindergarten-care*
