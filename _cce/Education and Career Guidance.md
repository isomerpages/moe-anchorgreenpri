The Education and Career Guidance (ECG) framework empowers students to explore opportunities, discover their purpose and stay relevant in a rapidly changing world. Guided by the school’s values and 21st Century Competencies (21CC), and anchored in a holistic “Head, Heart and Hand” approach, the framework nurtures confident, adaptable and purpose-driven individuals. This approach is integrated throughout our ECG experiences to support students’ academic, personal and professional growth. Through structured learning and authentic experiences, students explore diverse pathways, develop self-awareness and build practical skills—preparing them for future success.

<b>Lower Primary (P1–P3)</b> introduces foundational elements through the Head and Heart approaches. The Head component exposes students to diverse career possibilities, helping them identify interests and strengths while developing critical thinking. The <b>Heart</b> component focuses on self-awareness and social development, guiding students to recognise their strengths, values and goals, while fostering empathy and reflection.

<b>Upper Primary (P4–P6)</b> focuses on the Hand approach, where students engage in experiential learning. Through project-based tasks, collaboration and hands-on experiences, they develop practical skills such as communication, teamwork and problem-solving. Students apply their learning to real-world contexts, strengthening their readiness for future pathways and the world of work.
